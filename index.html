introduction of Pandokhar Dham

The world famous Shri Pandokhar Sarkar Dham is a miraculous pilgrimage site. Which is located in Pandokhar village of Datia district of Madhya Pradesh. In this Dham, a troubleshooter form of Lord Hanuman ji removes all the troubles. The Peethadheeshwar of this Dham, Param Pujya Gurudev Shri Gurusharan Ji Maharaj, removes the sorrows of the sad-suffering-critical and unhealthy people of the society by simple simple tips of Vedic and Shastras. With the blessings of Param Kripalu Pujyapad Sadgurudev Shri Hathiwanji Maharaj and the power of Lord Shri Pandokhar Sarkar (Shri Hanumant Balaji Maharaj), no one ever leaves this Dham with an empty bag. Whoever comes, all his wishes are fulfilled and all his sorrows are taken away. Trikaldarshi Gurudev writes any person's problem and its solution in the slip without asking and by the grace of Shri Hanumant Balaji Shri Pandokhar Sarkar, everyone's problems get resolved. From every new moon to full moon, Gurudev is present in Pandokhar Dham for the service of humanity and public welfare and diagnoses any problem like physical, mental, physical, divine, material problems and ghosts etc.

Devik - for troubleshooting

Pandokhar Sarkar solves any devik, ghost obstacle, tantra obstacle. If Pandokhar follows the rules laid down by the Sarkar, then he will surely get the benefit.

03

physical - for troubleshooting

For the solution of any physical problem by the Pandokhar Sarkar, you meet them and they know your problem and solution without telling you. Do your work according to the measures mentioned .
